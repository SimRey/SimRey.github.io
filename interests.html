Interests

A more personal side of Simone

“Lo qué uno quiere de verdad, es lo que está hecho para uno; entonces hay que tomarlo, o intentar: En eso se te puede ir la Vida, pero es una vida mucho mejor…”

- Mario Benedetti


Traveling is a profound passion that courses through my veins, drawing me to the captivating landscapes of the European continent, and Italy in particular, where my roots run deep. As I traverse new horizons, I cherish the opportunity to immerse myself in different cultures and languages, a skill that has enabled me to forge genuine connections with people from all walks of life. Amidst my explorations, I find tranquility and joy in leisurely walks and invigorating runs amidst nature's splendor. The beach, with its rhythmic waves and expansive vistas, holds a special place in my heart, offering a haven of serenity and inspiration. Through my linguistic abilities, I not only marvel at the tapestry of human expression but also gain profound insights into the intricacies of diverse traditions. This enriching journey, guided by my passions and curiosity, continuously fuels my desire to engage with the world and embrace the beauty that lies within its every corner.


When forced indoors, I tend to devote a significant amount of my attention to football, particularly the Premier League and La Liga. Additionally, I have a keen interest in reading, with literature from Hispanoamerica holding a special place in my heart and having left a lasting impression on me. Moreover, I spend a considerable amount of my leisure time delving into the latest technological breakthroughs in the domains of Machine Learning and Data Science, enrolling in courses and watching educational videos on YouTube.
